Universal Studios

Why you should go to Universal Studios.

This Theme Park has 3 parks to visit, Universal Studios, Islands of Adventure, and Volcano Bay. In the park Universal studios, there are rides and simulations that you can experience that are owned by the company Universal. Islands of Adventure of more thrill rides and scary rides than Universal Studios Park, and it is an experience for elder children. Volcano Bay is a waterpark that has many water slides, pools, and hotel resorts. Universal Studios is also very highly rated, and is a fun experince.

Disneyland is a themepark where there are many kid experinces to see, like toy story, or other kid movies that disney owns.
